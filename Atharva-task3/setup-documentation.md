## 4️⃣ Questions
Q: What is Hardhat?  
A: A local Ethereum development framework that lets you compile, test, and deploy contracts safely.

Q: Why do we use it?  
A: It creates a sandbox blockchain, handles accounts, gas, debugging, and scripts easily.
